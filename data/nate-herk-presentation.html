What's Working

1
Claude Code is the engine. 16 of 30 videos mention Claude Code. Average: 98K views. It's his core topic and the audience can't get enough.
2
"Master X% in Y Minutes" format crushes. Comprehensive beginner tutorials with time-bound promises (36 min, 28 min, 26 min) consistently break 100K+ views.
3
Comparison & honest review hooks. "100 Hours Testing..." (235K) works because it implies real effort and honest opinion. Builds massive trust.
4
Tangible outcomes in titles. "$10K Websites", "Beautiful Websites", "Executive Assistant" — specific results that viewers can picture getting themselves.
5
20-28 min sweet spot. 7 of the top 10 videos are 20-28 minutes. Long enough to deliver real value, short enough to finish watching.
6
"Just Changed Everything" / news hooks. Positioning updates as paradigm shifts ("Changed AI Automation Forever", "Just Added What Everyone Wanted") drives urgency.

What's Not Working

1
Business/client acquisition topics flop. "How to Sign Clients" and "Cold Email" videos avg 20-28K — 3x below channel average. Audience wants to BUILD, not sell.
2
Technical migration / maintenance content dies. "Migrate n8n Workflows Between Accounts" (5.6K) — operational, boring, no aspirational hook.
3
Guest/interview format underperforms. "$500K with Cold Email" (21K) — guest videos break the parasocial connection. Audience comes for Nate, not strangers.
4
Non-Claude tools get fraction of views. Cursor video: 3.2K. Pure n8n content averages ~49K vs Claude Code's 98K. The audience has picked a tribe.
5
"I Built..." without a clear outcome. "I Built an AI System That Automates My Proposals" (27K) — vague deliverable. Compare to "Building Beautiful Websites" (140K) which is specific.

Key Takeaways

Double down on Claude Code

53% of videos, 2x the views of any other topic. The audience is here for this. Every other topic he covers gets compared unfavorably.

Beginner-friendly tutorials are the growth engine

"Master 95%", "Teach a 10 Year Old", "Beginner's Guide" — these are the viral magnets. New viewers find these, subscribe, and stay.

n8n is declining but still relevant

n8n-only content (49K avg) underperforms vs Claude Code (98K avg). But "Claude Code + n8n" hybrid content (131K) bridges both audiences.

Stop making sales/biz advice videos

Business content (24K avg) tanks every time. His audience is technical builders, not salespeople. Use the podcast for those topics instead.
